Engineering tomorrow's technology.

  • FIRST® LEGO® League

    FLL® introduces young people to the fun and excitement of science and technology. Teams compete by programming an autonomous robot to score points on a thematic playing surface and creating innovative solutions to a problem, all while being guided by the FLL Core Values.

  • FLL WORLD CLASS Challengeâ„ 

    Every year, FLL® introduces a new challenge. This will include a new playing surface for the robot and a new project to research and create a solution. The Techneers will tackle this challenge.

  • LEGO® MINDSTORMS® EV3

    The kids will build and program a LEGO® MINDSTORMS® EV3 robot to complete as many of the challenges as possible in the competitions.

  • Colorado Springs

    The Techneers is made up of kids aged 9 - 14 who are located in the beautiful Cheyenne Canyon area of Colorado Springs.

news

After a very successful 2015 season, the Techneers are back at it. The kids are busy solving the 2016 FLL challenge.

2016-11-12: The Techneers compete in the 2016 regional challenge. It was a hard fought day overcoming problems with the robot, but the team scored very high in the project and the core values. To top it all off, they went home with the first place trophy in robot design!

2016-09-02: The 2016 challenge has been released. For today's meeting, the kids are busy putting the new challenge together.

2016-08-05: The fundraising has been going well, and we were able to purchase a third robot. This will mean more efficiency; more programming, less waiting for a robot to open up.

2016-07-24: The Techneers team is registered with FLL for the 2016 season! Our team number is 15527 (as it was in 2015).
