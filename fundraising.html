fundraising

For the robotics club (team) to function and be successful, we will need some equipment and supplies. The coaches, Kent and Deedle, are donating their time and there is no cost to join the club; however, robots and computers are expensive. Below, we have compiled 3 budgets: the first one is the bare minimum to get started; the second one would be ideal for us, as it would include the registration fees and everything we'd need for the year; the third one would be amazing, since it would allow for a second robot and laptop so different groups of kids could work on more than one task at the same time.

Levels of sponsorship:
  1. $5 - $99: Thank you card, team picture, and a surprise!
  2. $100+: All of the above plus your name on our team shirt.
