The Team

The team is comprised of boys and girls 9 - 14 years of age. The coaches will help guide the kids, but the work to complete the challanges and research the project is the responsibility of the kids.

Coaches

Deedle and Kent Murray are the team coaches. Deedle has many years of experience working with children (including time as owner of a preschool). Kent is a professional software programmer and has helped coach their kids' teams, including an FLL robotics team..
